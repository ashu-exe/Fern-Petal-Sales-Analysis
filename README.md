# Fern & Petal Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Fern & Petal sales data to identify revenue drivers, customer purchase behavior, seasonal trends, and business growth opportunities.

## Objective

To understand:

* sales trends across months and occasions
* top-performing products and categories
* customer buying behavior by time and city
* delivery performance and revenue relationships

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Pivot Charts
* Slicers / Interactive Dashboard

## Key Metrics

* Total Revenue: ₹35.2 Lakhs
* Total Orders: 1000
* Average Order Value: ₹3520.98
* Average Delivery Time: 5.53 Days

## Key Insights

* Sales are highly seasonal, with major peaks in February, August, and November.
* Occasion-led demand drives both order volume and revenue.
* Colors, Soft Toys, and Sweets are top-performing categories.
* Evening hours and weekends show stronger customer purchase activity.
* Revenue is concentrated in a few high-performing cities.

## Business Recommendations

* Increase inventory and marketing before peak gifting seasons.
* Promote bundled gift packs more aggressively.
* Run evening-targeted campaigns for better conversion.
* Improve logistics in top-performing cities.
* Launch off-season campaigns to reduce seasonality risk.
